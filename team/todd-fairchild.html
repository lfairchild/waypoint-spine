Todd A. Fairchild, M.D.

(Starting in mid-January 2019, Dr. Fairchild will be seeing patients primarily at the new Wausau clinic.
Dr. Fairchild will hold limited office hours in Stevens Point as well.)

Dr. Fairchild grew up in Muncie, Indiana and graduated from Harvard College. He received his medical degree from Yale University. Dr. Fairchild completed his residency in orthopedic surgery at the University of Iowa, followed by a fellowship at Twin Cities Spine Center in Minneapolis, a nationally recognized center of excellence in spine surgery.

Dr. Fairchild has lived and practiced spinal surgery in central Wisconsin for almost ten years. Outside of work, Dr. Fairchild and his wife Amy enjoy watching their children play in local youth sports programs. Dr. Fairchild also enjoys reading fiction, watching movies with his family, and undertaking small construction projects.

“I treat all patients how I want my family to be treated. I first sit down with patients and try to figure out how they can maximize non-operative treatment and avoid surgery. If all non-operative treatments have failed and if I am able to clearly identify the anatomic abnormality causing pain, I discuss the risk and benefits of spine surgery. If we proceed with surgery, I utilize the most modern techniques and instrumentation, including minimally invasive procedures.”

Todd A. Fairchild, M.D.
